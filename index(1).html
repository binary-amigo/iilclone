SERVICES

Our Offerings

Courses

IIL provides a variety of useful courses based upon the needs of changing time and helps students to cope up with the modern competitive world. Our courses primarily deals with career, personality, life skills, management skills, and more.

Counseling

Team IIL provides the facility of one on one counseling sessions for students to guide them towards the path of success. this facility is absolutely free for all the students from our partner institutes.

Seminars

To keep students inspired in their life, IIL organizes so many inspirational seminars, webinars and educational sessions on a regular basis.

Learning Forum

IIL creates a great learning environment for all the students in your institute by providing a free learning platform to them where they can learn so many new and valuable things daily.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is IIL? IIL is a platform for students to learn and grow. IIL provides a variety of useful courses in schools for the overall development of students. These courses are based upon the needs of changing time and helps students to cope up with the modern competitive world. Our courses primarily deals with career, personality, life skills, management skills, health, and more.
How to enrol in your courses? You can not directly enrol in our courses. You can contact our partner institutes to enrol in the courses or you can contact us at Mail or Call us. If you are in any school then mention your school name in the mail or send us a query regarding that, we will contact your institute and you can enrol in our courses.
Our Vision and Mission? Our vision is to make Indian youth aware of their maximum potential, so that they can lead a better life and set an example for their acquaintances and also inspire them towards a good life. We are on our mission to create Leaders throughout the India.
How to call us in your school? You can contact us via Contact details mentioned in the Contact section of the website.
